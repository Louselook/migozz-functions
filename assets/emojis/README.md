# Emoji Assets

## Add Your Emoji Images Here

Place your emoji images in this folder with the following names:

1. `emoji_1.png` - Sunglasses emoji (yellow with purple sunglasses)
2. `emoji_2.png` - Winking emoji
3. `emoji_3.png` - Cool emoji with sunglasses
4. `emoji_4.png` - Happy emoji
5. `emoji_5.png` - Star emoji
6. `emoji_6.png` - Play button (pink/red)
7. `emoji_7.png` - Heart emoji
8. `emoji_8.png` - Camera/photo emoji (orange)
9. `emoji_9.png` - Instagram-style emoji (pink/purple)
10. `emoji_10.png` - At symbol emoji (blue)

## Image Requirements

- Format: PNG (with transparency recommended)
- Recommended size: 512x512 pixels or higher
- The images will be displayed as background with 15% opacity

## After Adding Images

Run: `flutter pub get`
Then: Hot reload or restart your app
